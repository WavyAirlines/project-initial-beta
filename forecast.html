About us

The Smart beach project, spearheaded by the Nuclear Innovation Institue's Municipal Innovation Council (MIC) in collaboration with local stakeholders, aims to revolutionize beach safety in Kincardine, Ontario. The initiative comes in response to a community-driven desire to enhance safety at Station Beach, which has experiencesd unfortunate incidents over the past decade. The Smart Beach project holds immense impotance for Kincardine as it strives to provide beachgoers with real-time information on water conditions, thus significantly improving safety at Station Beach
